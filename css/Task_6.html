Packing Tips

  • Always pack light; you don't need as much as you think.
  • Roll your clothes instead of folding them to save space.
  • Bring a portable charger for your devices.
  • Don't forget your sunscreen and medications.
  • Pack a travel pillow for long flights or train rides.
